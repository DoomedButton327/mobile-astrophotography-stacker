// CORE CAPABILITIES

Built for the Night Sky

Star Alignment

Automatically detects and aligns stars across all frames using sub-pixel precision algorithms. Corrects for rotation, translation, and atmospheric drift between shots.

AUTO ALIGN

Image Stacking

Stack dozens to hundreds of exposures using Mean, Median, Kappa-Sigma, or Weighted Average methods to dramatically reduce noise while preserving faint nebulosity and star color.

NOISE REDUCTION

Sky Gradient Removal

Intelligent light pollution and gradient correction removes uneven sky glow from urban shooting locations, recovering shadow detail and natural color balance.

LIGHT POLLUTION

Foreground Composition

Separate foreground and sky stacking lets you combine a sharp landscape with a deep-sky stacked background — perfect for Milky Way landscape composites.

LANDSCAPE MODE

Hot Pixel Removal

Automatically detects and eliminates hot pixels, amp glow, and sensor artifacts from long-exposure astrophotography frames before stacking.

ARTIFACT FIX

16-bit Output

Exports full 16-bit TIFF files with maximum dynamic range for post-processing in Lightroom, Photoshop, PixInsight, or any image editor of your choice.
